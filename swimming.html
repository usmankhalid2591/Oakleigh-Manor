Our 3 Types of Swimming Pool

Ceramic tiled concrete shell pool

Ceramic tiled pools are perfect for high specification and uniquely shaped commercial or domestic pool owners, essentially formed in a 300mm thick reinforced concrete shell, all of which have 3 waterproofing systems and have a lifetime expectancy of several decades.

Our Ceramic tiled pools can be made to any shape or depth, with built in beach ends, full width steps, circular formats and infinity edges.

Rectangular or square shaped pools can be fitted with an Aquamatic Safety cover hidden under the pool terrace, most pool heating, pool lighting and diving boards are available with this pool type.

A Liner pool with blockwork shell

Liner pools are perfect for domestic pool owners, formed with a format of concrete floor and blockwork walls, all of which are lined with a perfectly fitted highly durable PVC liner.

All liner pools have a huge choice of plain or patterned finishes and have the option of underlay thermal and comfort foam making your pool both energy efficient and comfortable under the foot.

Our liner pools can be made to any rectangular or square shape or depth, with built in beach ends and full width steps.

Rectangular or square shaped liner pools can be fitted with an Aquamatic Safety cover hidden under the pool terrace, most pool heating, pool lighting and diving boards are available with this pool type.

Ask our friendly pool team for more detailsâ€¦..

Combination Jacuzzi & Swim pool

Combination Spa Pools are perfect for domestic pool owners who require both the luxury of a Jacuzzi Spa combined with a small pool for areas or gardens with limited space.

The combination pools come in a pre-formed fibreglass shell, with built in pumps, filters and plant room equipment simplifying the site installation.

The Pools are fitted with swim jets to enable exercise swimming against the current or flow, whilst the Jacuzzi sections are fitted with massage and aromatherapy luxury jets to give you the ability to exercise then relax.

Standard Pool Plant & Equipment for Ceramic Tiled & Liner Pools

All Ceramic tiled & Liner Pools require a standard plant room and equipment to circulate and filter your pool water to maintain a healthy water environment.

We will fit your Plant room with a suitable electrical supply and control panel, together with a circulation pump and and Glass Media filtration system with backwash facility to help you maintain clear and healthy water.

You may also consider additional UV Filtration options, Heating options or Auto Dosers which provide automatic chemical top-ups to your pool.

Ask our friendly team for more details.

Other great options for your Pool

Automatic Safety Pool Covers are a fantastic Pool Cover system, providing ultimate child safety, thermal and heat loss reduction together with highly reduced pool cleaning & maintenance.

By the turn of a key, your pool cover will completely close the pool from the outside world, preventing anyone from being able to fall into your pool water, all of which can be located and hidden under your pool terrace.

Available in 8 different colour options our Automatic Safety Covers provide a stylish and crisp appearance for most Pool Gardens together with ultimate safety for your family. Ask our friendly pool team for more detailsâ€¦.

Eco Air Source Heat Pumps are one of the most energy efficient way to heat your pool during the swimming season of April to October.

Our heat pumps can save up to 80% on heating costs for your pool in comparison with standard electric heaters (Electro Heaters) and have also been proven to be more efficient than natural gas or oil fired heating systems.

Our design team will select the correct heater size for your pool, then ensure your electricity supply to the plant room is sufficient to supply your new heat pump.

Ask our friendly team for more details.

Our Pool lights are a fantastic addition to any pool, not only illuminating the pool for evening swims but enhancing the appearance of your pool from inside your home.

We provide a huge range of low voltage halogen or LED lights, either for underwater flood illumination or decorative marker lights which highlight the architecture and shape of the pool, marking benches, steps & pool stylisation.

Our Ceramic Tiled Pools are able to fit as many lights as the pool design requires, yet many light fittings are available for our Liner Pools.

Our standard Automation Equipment

Our automated installations come with the following equipment fitted as standard.


Remote Control Keyfobs

Programmed uniquely to your gate system. Working range approx 30m. Giving you the ability to control your gates from you hand.

Underground Motor System

IP68 Fully waterproof to submerged level. “Manual Release System” for power cuts. 2 Year Manufacturer Guarantee.

x Pair of Photocells (Closing phase)

The first of our standard safety features. These create a beam across your entrance which stops the gates from closing whilst trafficking the opening.

Emergency Stop Button

Fitted as standard as an additional safety feature. Stops the gates immediately during any movement. Normally fitted on the rear side of the gates.

The Control Box

Mounted on the Rear side of one of your gate posts/piers. Full Waterproof and secure.

Gate stop

Fitted as standard on all pairs of swing gates, your gates shut tight and secure onto a centre stop adding extra security.

Our Intercom options

This gate system is fully compatible with any of the following intercoms, fitted alongside your standard automation system.


Wireless Voice Intercom

Gate opening ability from the house. Operating range up to 70m. No communication cabling required. Installed Price from £749 inc Vat.

Wireless Voice Intercom + PIN Entry Keypad

Gate opening ability from the house. Allows PIN code gate access. No communication cabling required. Operating range up to 70m. Installed Price from £799 inc Vat.

Colour Video Intercom

Provides video communication between gates & house monitor. Gate opening ability from the house. Installed Price from £899 inc Vat(subject to cabling).

Colour Video Intercom + PIN Entry Keypad

Provides video communication between gates & house monitor. Gate opening ability from the house. Installed Price from £949 inc Vat (subject to cabling) .

GSM Intercom + PIN Entry Keypad

Gate communication & opening ability a mobile phone. Allows PIN Code gate access. Installed Price from £989 inc Vat

Gate stop

Fitted as standard on all pairs of swing gates, your gates shut tight and secure onto a centre stop adding extra security.

Additional Safety options

These additional safety options are all compatible with your standard automated system. Here at Oakleigh Manor, safety comes first, so we are happy to fit any additional safety devices if you feel there are people at risk.


Anti-Crush Safety Edges(Closing phase)

These sensors can be fitted at any potential crush points within your gate movement. Installed Price from £449 inc Vat.

x Pair of Photocells (Opening phase)

The first of our standard safety features. Fitted onto posts, these stop the gates from opening when the beam is blocked. Installed Price from £349 inc Vat.

Flashing Light

This illuminates for a few seconds before very gate movement, warning the users of a new gate movement. Installed Price from £199 inc Vat.

Additional Automation options

All our standard automation gate systems can be fitted with any of these additional options, tailoring your gates to suit you.


GSM Gate Opener

This option allows you to open your gates from a mobile phone, from anywhere in the world. Installed Price from £439 inc Vat

Push Button Gate Opener

A Push Button can be fitted on any side of the gates to give users the ability to open the gates. Installed Price from £149 inc Vat

Timer Control

Timers can be fitted to provide timed openings, or with Push Button Openers or Vehicle Loop Detectors. Installed Price from £219 inc Vat

Key Switch Opener

This can be fitted either side of your gates to provide keyholders with gate opening control. Installed Price from £199 inc Vat

Vehicle Loop Detector

This system is installed under the road/driveway surface, when auto open is required when you drive up to the gates in a vehicle. Installed Price from £869 inc Vat

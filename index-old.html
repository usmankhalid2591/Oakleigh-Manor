We Design & Build Exquisite Outdoor Spaces

Oakleigh Manor was founded in 1996, since then has evolved into a multi disciplined team of talented professionals, incorporating a Landscape & Garden Design Team, highly skilled Landscape & Garden Construction Craftsmen, Garden Joinery, Irrigation, Lighting & Automation Engineers and a caring team of Garden Maintenance & Grounds Maintenance staff who provide ongoing Aftercare and Maintenance.

Everything required to create a stunning Garden – Landscape – External space Starting out as a bespoke Garden Design & Build Company, over the last three decades Oakleigh Manor now provides a vast range of high quality services, all aimed a creating beautiful Gardens, Landscapes & External spaces.

Where we operate

Oakleigh Manor commonly carry out projects throughout London, Surrey, Sussex, Kent & Essex, serving private clients, contractors, independent designers and the public sector. The company has also been involved in bespoke projects throughout the wider region of the UK and within Eastern Europe.

Award Winning Landscape & Garden Designers

Oakleigh Manor’s talented team of Garden & Landscape Designers, lead by Stuart Barten and Ross Gandon, the team develop and design beautiful gardens for homeowners, developers & property owners who’s sole aim will be to maximise the potential of your space, bespoke to suit each client’s style, taste & personal requirements.

READ MORE →

Highly Skilled Craftsmen

Oakleigh Manor’s ethos of creating high quality and functional outdoor spaces has led the company to numerous national awards, the company maintains fully employed skilled Landscape Craftsmen teams including hard and soft landscapers, together with an in house Garden Joinery Department, Landscape Lighting, Irrigation & Gate Automation Engineers.

Impeccable Standards & Workmanship

All Oakleigh Manor projects are delivered with our seal of quality and guarantee. Our multi-talented in-house teams ensure we can deliver our high end product standards, on time, and within budget, with caring project managers who nurse every client through the journey of their landscape project.

Simply complete our online Enquiry Form or call the Oakleigh Manor team to discuss your requirements. Our team can guide you through your options available within all our services, provide initial budgets or arrange an Initial Site Visit from one of our friendly team.

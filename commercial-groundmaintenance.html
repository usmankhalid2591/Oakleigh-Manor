Oakleigh Manor Grounds Maintenance Services

The Oakleigh Manor Horticultural team provides Grounds Maintenance to the public sector and commercial clients, throughout London, Surrey, Sussex, Kent and Essex. Our in-house specialist team provides periodical maintenance for all landscape areas to ensure the grounds are being maintained to a suitable and desired standard.

Oakleigh Manor understands the importance of first impressions. Our philosophy is to deliver care, passion and excellence in grounds maintenance, creating environmentally sound spaces.

If you require Grounds Maintenance Services, contact our friendly Horticultural Team on 0800 023 1310, or simply complete an enquiry form.

About our service

All our maintenance contracts and services are tailored to suit the individual site requirements, scheduled into a reliable annual ground care plan. Our staff are professional, friendly and experienced, so you will feel confident that your grounds are being cared for in the correct way.

Oakleigh Manor has accumulated national awards for our service, which pay testament to our quest for continuous high quality service, product and aftercare. We currently hold Grounds Maintenance contracts at prestigious, domestic and commercial sites. Our current Commercial clients include Holiday Extra’s Head Office Grounds and the Environment Agency Thames Barrier and associated sites. At the Environment Agency our team maintains all areas, from the restricted and secure compounds to the large expanses open to the general public. Oakleigh Manor ensures the same high quality of service is provided in our Commercial sites, as is delivered to our domestic clients, where our company first began

If you would like to arrange an initial 1-hour, free of charge meeting with one of our Head Gardeners, then please contact our friendly office team. Within this meeting we can provide an initial health and condition check of the grounds, and discuss your requirements in more detail.

Once the meeting is complete, we will process fixed fee proposals for any contracted or one off works requested, and draw up an on going ground care proposal for you to consider.

How We Work…

Heres a brief (in a nutshell) description of what to expect.

If you would like to speak to one of our team about your landscape requirements, one of our team will call you to discuss your initial thoughts or we can arrange to visit you at your home or site, free of charge, for an Initial Site Visit. This meeting will normally take an hour of your time.

During your Initial Site Meeting, firstly we will listen aim to fully understand your requirements, then give you our professional opinion and guidance for the type of work you require.

If your work is of a large scale or complex nature, and you have not had any previous design guidance, we will offer Design Services, who the design service works, timescales, feasibility and budgetary advice. Our design proposals are offered at no obligation, our fees are explained and agreed prior to any commission.

If your works do not require Design Input, we will provide you with a no obligation fixed fee proposal for the works, together with a schedule of work and payment structure.

All our disciplines are clear & transparent, we agree any costs up front, our specifications are based on high quality craftsmanship and end product, with guarantee and longevity to protect your investment.

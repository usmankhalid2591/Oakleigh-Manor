Garden Maintenance by Oakleigh Manor

Oakleigh Manor Horticultural Services is a comprehensive garden maintenance and horticultural company, providing services for all aspects of your garden. Our dedicated and professional team will guarantee quality and ensure our expertise is translated into your garden.

If you require Garden Maintenance Services, contact our friendly Horticultural Team on 01227 750016 , or simply complete an enquiry form.

Our Professional Garden Maintenance Services

Our Garden Maintenance team also provide specialised Garden Maintenance services for individual parts of your garden that matter to you.

Seasonal Lawn Care Plans

  • Seasonal Lawn Treatments- Spring, Summer, Autumn & Winter.
  • Weed and feed treatments at the correct time of year to encourage a strong and healthy lawn.
  • Scarification and hollow tining.

Horticultural Border Care

  • Specialised pruning, using the best Horticultural practises.
  • Granular and liquid feeds applied at the correct times of year.
  • Regular plant health checks to monitor for early signs of pest and disease.
  • Herbicide and pesticide treatment as required.

Hedge Cutting

  • Professional Hedge Cutting at the correct times of year, to maintain shape and form.
  • Inclusive of all Machinery and Equipment required.
  • Waste materials removed from your garden, if required.

Specialist Pruning & Training

  • Specialised Pruning & Training, using the best Horticultural practises.
  • Installation of high tension stainless steel wires & trellis systems.
  • Wisteria pruning every Winter & Summer.

Patio Cleaning

  • Moss and Algae removal using specially selected, environmentally friendly products.
  • Herbicide treatments as required.
  • High pressure water cleaning.

Planting Schemes

  • Detailed & planting schemes created by our Lead Horticulturalist.
  • Soil preparation.
  • Planting beds enriched with organic matter, if required.

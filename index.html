We Design & Build Exquisite Outdoor Spaces

Oakleigh Manor was created in 1996, since then the company has evolved into a multi-disciplined team of talented professionals. Incorporating Landscape and Gardening Design Teams, highly skilled Landscape and Garden Construction Craftsmen, Garden Joinery, Irrigation Services, Drainage Services, Lighting and Automation Engineers. The company also provides teams of Garden Maintenance and Ground Maintenance staff who provide on-going aftercare and maintenance for clients.

Everything required to create a stunning Garden – Landscape – External Spaces from one unique single source Oakleigh Manor.

Where we Operate

Oakleigh Manor operates and delivers projects throughout the South East including London South and South East, Kent, East Sussex and areas of Surrey and Essex, servicing private clients, contractors, independent designers and the public sector.

Award Winning Landscape and Garden Design

Oakleigh Manor’s talented team of Garden Landscapers and Designers are lead by Stuart Barten and Ross Gandon, under there combined experiences the Oakleigh Manor team design and develop beautiful garden spaces for homeowner’s, developer’s and property owners. The sole aim is to maximise the potential of these spaces, designed to bespoke standards to suit each client’s style, taste and personal requirements.

Award Winning Pedigree

Numerous awards have rewarded Oakleigh Manor’s ethos of creating high quality and functional outdoor spaces. The company maintains teams of highly skilled craftsman in hard and soft landscaping, together with an in-house Garden Joinery Department, Landscape Lighting and Gate Automation Engineers.

Impeccable Standards and Workmanship

All Oakleigh Manor projects are delivered with our seal of quality and a guarantee assurance. Our multi-disciplined in-house teams ensure the delivery of high-end standards, on time, within budgets through the guidance of our Project Managers who assist clients through the delivery journey of their landscape projects.

Simply complete our online Enquiry Form or call the Oakleigh Manor team to discuss your requirements. Our team can guide you through the options available for all our services, provide initial budgets or arrange an Initial Site Visit from one of our friendly team.
